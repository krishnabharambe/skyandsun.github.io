Hair Treatment

Hair care is an overall term for hygiene and cosmetology involving the hair which grows from the human scalp, and to a lesser extent facial, pubic and other body hair. Hair care routines differ according to an individual's culture and the physical characteristics of one's hair.

Face Care

A facial is a family of skin care treatments for the face, including steam, exfoliation, extraction, creams, lotions, facial masks, peels, and massage. They are normally performed in beauty salons, but are also a common spa treatment. They are used for general skin health as well as for specific skin conditions.

Menicure/Pedicure

A manicure is a cosmetic beauty treatment for the fingernails and hands performed at home or in a nail salon. ... When the same is applied to the toenails and feet, the treatment is referred to as a pedicure.
